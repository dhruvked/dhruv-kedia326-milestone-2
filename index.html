LINE-EQUATION: y=mx+b.

Where y and x are points on a line & m is the slope whereas b is the Y-intercept.

Slope=It is basically the change in the y-coordinate of a point on the line with respect to the x-coordinate.

For Example:- Assume first point is (2,3) and slope is 2. Then after x increases by 1, y increaases by 2.

Y-intercept=It is the y-coordinate of a point on the line when the x-coordinate is ZERO

For Example:-In the previous example, we know x,y and slope. We can calculate the b and find that it is y of the point on the line when x=0 i.e when it goes through the y-axis.
